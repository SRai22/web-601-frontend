How to Adopt a Pet

Adopting a pet is a rewarding experience. Follow these steps to make sure both you and the pet are ready for a lifelong match.

Adoption Steps

  1. Visit the shelter and meet the pets.
  2. Ask about medical history and temperament.
  3. Complete an adoption application.
  4. Prepare your home and welcome your new pet.

What to Bring

When you come to adopt, bring a valid ID and any information about your current pets or living situation.
